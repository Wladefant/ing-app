# **1\. Main Presentation (10–13 min)**

## **Intro — Sofia & Mateo, Berkay (2 min)**

**Berkay:**

Today, we want to start not with a product — but with a problem.  
 In Germany, **1 in 5 young people is in debt**.  
 At the same time, over **90% of teenagers say they didn’t receive proper financial education in school**.

**Mateo:**

This creates a clear gap between what people need to know about money — and what they are actually taught.

And instead of learning from reliable sources,  
many young people turn to social media \-  
where so-called “finfluencers” often promote risky or misleading advice,  
just to gain views or profit from their audience.

As a result, this gap leads to poor financial decisions in real life.

**Sofia:**

That’s why we created ING Leo \- an AI assistant integrated into the ING banking app,  
that not only helps you manage money, but teaches you how to do it from the very beginning.

It tracks your financial behavior in real time, and adapts to you \- giving personalized advice based on your actions.

## **Transition — Berkay (1 min)**

We didn’t just build an app.  
 We built a **system that grows with the user**.

Most banks start when you already have money.  
 We start when you are just learning how money works.

That’s why we created two modes:  
 **Leo Junior** — for learning  
 and **Leo Pro** — for real life.

And they are connected by one key moment — your **18th birthday**.

## **Vova — Kids App (5-6 min)**

Let’s see how this works in practice.

Meet Lili — she is 15 years old.  
 When she opens the app, she doesn’t see a boring banking interface.  
 She sees a **financial simulator**.

### **Salary & Taxes**

Leo gives Lili a virtual salary.  
 But immediately — taxes are deducted.

She asks:  
 *“Where did my money go?”*

And Leo explains taxes instantly \- in simple, clear language.

### **Investing (no risk)**

Lili can also invest — but with **virtual money**.

She buys stocks using **real market data**, but without any risk.

If her stock drops, Leo doesn’t just show a loss \-  
 it explains **why it happened**.

### **Quizzes & Learning**

To keep users engaged, we added gamification:

* daily quizzes  
* leaderboards  
* achievements and rewards

And most importantly —  
 we can run **live quiz battles**, similar to Kahoot.

### **Conclusion**

So instead of learning theory, kids learn by doing, experimenting, and making mistakes — safely. Learning from real-world data and real situations, not from outdated textbooks.

**Sofia — Adult App (5-7 min)**

Then comes the key moment.  
 Lili turns 18\.

**Game Mode transforms into Real Mode.**

Now, this is no longer a simulation \- it’s real money.

**AI Agents**

In the adult version, Leo offers three types of AI agents:

1. a personal accountant,  
2. a personal investor,  
3. and an interactive AI chat.

#### **1\. Personal Accountant**

Leo becomes a personal accountant:

* tracks all transactions  
* categorizes spending  
* gives proactive suggestions

For example:  
 *“You’re spending a lot on subscriptions \- want to cancel some?”*

#### **2\. Personal Investor** The second agent is a personal investor. It doesn’t just show stocks \- it helps your money work for you.

suggests ETFs, stocks, and bonds  
adapts to your risk level  
explains every decision

It also analyzes market news in real time,  
so its recommendations are always based on the latest information.

You can even enable automated investing,  
so your money doesn’t just sit — it keeps working for you.

All decisions are driven by data, not emotions.

**3\. AI Chat & Widgets**  
The third agent is an interactive AI chat.

You can ask any question about finance or economics  
and get clear answers instantly.

But it doesn’t just explain things —  
it helps you use your banking app faster and easier.

The chat also includes smart widgets,  
helping you visualize your data and understand complex topics.

For example:  
1)want to send money to Ben?  
Just type it in the chat \-  
one click, and it’s done.

2)Or want to understand your investments better?  
Just ask Leo in the chat \-  
and it will analyze your portfolio,  
showing what’s performing well  
and what you might consider buying or selling.

3)Or want to understand your spending better?  
Just ask Leo in the chat —  
and it will analyze your expenses  
and give you a clear overview of your finances.

So instead of searching for answers or navigating menus,  
you just ask \- and Leo takes care of it.

 **Additional Value**

Leo also:

* explains contracts and bills  
* helps international users   
* provides 24/7 financial Ai chat support

### **Big Idea**

We are moving towards a society with:

less confusion  
more financial literacy  
smarter financial decisions

**Mateo & Berkay — Legal (1–2 min)**

**Mateo:**

When it comes to AI in banking, the biggest concern is trust.  
 People ask: *“Is my data safe?”*

**Berkay:**

That’s why Leo is built fully aligned with **GDPR and the EU AI Act**.

* no open internet learning  
* only anonymized internal data  
* full user control

Leo can suggest actions — but it can never execute them without your approval.

**Mateo:**

For minors, we add extra safeguards:

* parental consent  
* age verification  
* simplified explanations

Now let’s look at how we built this from a technical perspective.

We used an approach called **vibe coding**.

###  **What is Vibe Coding**

Instead of spending weeks designing static screens in Figma,

### **Tools & Approach**

We used modern tools such as:

* AI-assisted interface generation  
* real-time prototyping

Unlike Figma:

We don’t just show how it looks \-
