## NOTES FOR SLIDE DESIGN

- **Colors:** ING orange (#FF6200) for headers and accents. White/dark backgrounds.
- **Font:** Keep it large. Max 5-6 lines per slide. Managers sit far from the screen.
- **Screenshots:** Use actual app screenshots — they are more convincing than mockups.
- **Live demo slides (10, 15):** These are placeholders — switch to the live app here.
- **Animations:** Minimal. Let the content speak. The birthday transition is the only wow-animation.
- **Language:** All slides in English. Keep text simple — German-speaking audience.
