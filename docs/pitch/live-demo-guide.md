# ING Leo — Live Demo Guide
*Step-by-step walkthrough for the 10-minute live demo portion*

---

## Before the Demo

### Setup Checklist
- [ ] App running locally or on a demo server
- [ ] Browser open, app loaded on Junior dashboard
- [ ] Leo chat closed (will open during demo)
- [ ] Screen shared / projector connected
- [ ] Stable internet connection (needed for AI responses and stock data)
- [ ] Quiz battle link ready (if doing live audience quiz)
- [ ] Have backup: pre-recorded demo scenarios in case of API issues

### Demo Accounts Ready
- **Junior account**: "Lili", age 15, some XP and badges already earned
- **Adult account**: Real-looking ING interface with transaction history

---

## Demo Flow (10 minutes)

### BLOCK 1: Junior App (5 min)

**Step 1 — Dashboard Overview (30 sec)**
1. Show the Junior dashboard
2. Point out: XP bar, level, streak counter, badges
3. Say: *"This is what Lili sees when she opens the app. Not a bank — a game."*

**Step 2 — Salary & Taxes (1 min)**
1. Navigate to salary screen
2. Show the virtual salary being received
3. Show tax deductions appearing
4. Open Leo chat → ask *"Why are taxes taken from my salary?"*
5. Show Leo's explanation in simple language
6. Say: *"Most adults cannot explain this clearly. Leo can — in seconds."*

**Step 3 — Investment Simulator (1.5 min)**
1. Navigate to investment screen
2. Show the list of real stocks with real prices
3. Buy a stock (e.g., Apple) with virtual money
4. Show the portfolio view
5. Open Leo → ask *"Should I invest in Tesla right now?"*
6. Show Leo's AI analysis (news scanning, sector assessment)
7. Say: *"Real market data. Real analysis. Zero risk."*

**Step 4 — Quiz (1 min)**
1. Navigate to quiz screen
2. Start a quick quiz (AI-generated)
3. Answer 2-3 questions
4. Show XP earned, badge progress
5. Say: *"Every quiz is unique — generated by AI. You cannot just memorize answers."*

**Step 5 — Live Quiz Battle (1 min) [OPTIONAL]**
1. Open Kahoot-style quiz
2. Share join link with audience
3. Run 3-4 quick questions
4. Show live leaderboard
5. Say: *"This is how we make financial education fun — and social."*

> **FALLBACK**: If time is short or WiFi is unreliable, skip the live quiz and just show the screen.

---

### TRANSITION: Birthday Animation (30 sec)

1. Trigger the 18th birthday transition
2. Let the animation play (confetti, mode change)
3. Say: *"Lili is 18. Game over — real life begins."*
4. Pause — let the visual impact land

---

### BLOCK 2: Adult App (4.5 min)

**Step 6 — Adult Dashboard (30 sec)**
1. Show the adult dashboard
2. Say: *"Now it looks familiar, right? This is your app — the ING app — with Leo added."*
3. Point out: real account names (Girokonto, Extra Konto, Depot)

**Step 7 — Transaction Analysis (1 min)**
1. Show transaction list
2. Show categorized spending (pie chart view)
3. Show Leo's proactive insight: *"You spent 47€ on delivery this week"*
4. Say: *"Leo finds patterns you would not see on your own."*

**Step 8 — Subscription Manager (1 min)**
1. Navigate to subscriptions
2. Show detected unused subscription
3. Show AI-generated cancellation email
4. Say: *"One click — Leo writes the email. The user just sends it."*

**Step 9 — AI Chat Widgets (1.5 min)**

Open Leo chat and demonstrate three scenarios:

**Widget 1 — Transfer:**
1. Type: *"Send 50 euros to Ben"*
2. Show transfer widget appearing in chat
3. Say: *"No menus. No forms. Just type and confirm."*

**Widget 2 — Spending:**
1. Type: *"Show me my spending this month"*
2. Show spending chart widget
3. Say: *"Your finances, visualized — inside the chat."*

**Widget 3 — Portfolio:**
1. Type: *"How are my investments doing?"*
2. Show stock/portfolio analysis widget
3. Say: *"Leo reads the market, analyzes your portfolio, and gives you clear recommendations."*

**Step 10 — Stock Deep Dive (30 sec)**
1. Navigate to a stock detail page
2. Show AI analysis panel
3. Say: *"Leo scans hundreds of news articles in real time. Recommendations based on data, not emotions."*

## Demo Recovery Plans

### If the AI is slow to respond:
- Say: *"Leo is thinking — just like you would when analyzing 200 news articles."*
- Move to the next feature, come back when it loads

### If the AI gives an unexpected response:
- Say: *"This is a live AI — every response is generated in real time. That is what makes it powerful — and sometimes unpredictable."*
- Ask a different question

### If the internet drops:
- Switch to pre-loaded demo scenarios (demo-scenarios.ts has 25+ pre-built conversations)
- Say: *"Let me show you a saved example of what Leo can do."*

### If the quiz battle does not work:
- Show the quiz screen with a solo quiz instead
- Skip the audience participation part

---

## Key Phrases to Use During Demo

Keep language **simple and clear** for German-speaking audience:

| Instead of... | Say... |
|--------------|--------|
| "Machine learning model" | "The AI" or "Leo" |
| "Function calling API" | "Leo can take actions inside the app" |
| "Natural language processing" | "You just type what you want" |
| "Real-time data integration" | "Leo uses live market data" |
| "Gamification mechanics" | "Points, levels, and rewards" |
| "Conversational banking" | "You talk to your bank — and it talks back" |
| "Portfolio diversification" | "Spreading your money across different investments" |
